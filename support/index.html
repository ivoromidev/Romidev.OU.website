Support

Need help with Notehand? We're here. Notehand is a handwritten note-taking app for your Google Calendar meetings — write by hand, highlight what matters, and recap your day or week.

Contact

Email us at support@romidev.ee.

We aim to reply within a few business days. Include your device model and iPadOS version if you're reporting a problem.

Quick help

Notehand requires a Google account to sync your calendar. Your notes stay on your device — they are not shared or uploaded to our servers.

  • Sign in with Google to see your calendar meetings as ready-to-write pages.
  • Write with Apple Pencil. Mark decisions, actions, and important moments as you go.
  • Browse Highlights to recap your day or week, or search by person and meeting.
